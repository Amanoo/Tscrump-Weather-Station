About

Make Weather Stations Great Again! is a school project made by the Tscrump Group. Made out of basic components such as a Temperature and Air Pressure Sensor, A Brightness Sensor, Humidity Sensor and a large scale Data Network.

Wether you are a granny or a little kid, everyone likes to see what kind of weather there is comming. Control the weather station at home or do it while being abroad. You can check the weather in your home town by using this site wich uses the LORA network.
